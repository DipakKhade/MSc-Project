What is Brain Hemorrhage

A brain hemorrhage, also known as cerebral hemorrhage or intracerebral hemorrhage, is a medical condition characterized by bleeding within the brain tissue. This bleeding can result from various causes, such as a ruptured blood vessel, head trauma, high blood pressure, or certain medical conditions like an aneurysm or arteriovenous malformation. The presence of blood in the brain can lead to increased pressure, damage to brain cells, and potentially life-threatening complications.
